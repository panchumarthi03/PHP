    Wild Circus presents its futuristic show "The Wild - Reinvent the Circus"
    Free your mind, let yourself be carried to the future and watch the
    most incredible artists evolve with the latest technological innovations.
    A huge infrastructure, more than 100 technicians accompany 42 artists
    to create the dreams of more than 4000 people every night. After
    traveling all over the world, in 2016 the Wild Circus becomes resident
    for 5 years on the stage of the Caesar Palace in Las Vegas for the most
    visionary and ambitious show ever imagined. Between robots,holograms,
    3D projections and walls of water, come to discover the circus of tomorrow.

    This creations have been acclaimed by millions of viewers and have thousands
    of performances in many cities around the world. In addition to its touring shows,
    more than 1,500 custom-designed events have been performed around the world.
    We have an fantastic and unique show in the world by the fabulous tamer in the
    "Cirque Pinder", Frédéric Edelstein! The show have not less 12 white lions, a race
    in danger of extinction, he saves their lives in Africa. Frédéric pass a part of this life
    to draw up they dangerous animals to create an breathtaking spectacle!

    Wait!That's not all! We have a lot amazing shows to show you, we reserve a lot of surprises. For 20 years, the Wild Circus always ready for a challenge to satisfy and impress you.And you, are you ready to see the most wonderful show of your life?
